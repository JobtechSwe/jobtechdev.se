Our focus areas!

Labor market data

We create, collect and quality-assure data to give people and companies better conditions to develop in the labor market.

Language Data

We create a common language for the Swedish labor market so that people and companies can easily find each other.

Personal data

We create ways for individuals in the labor market to own, use, and move their own data easily.
